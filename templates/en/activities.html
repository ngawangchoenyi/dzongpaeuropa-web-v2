Activities

Meditation, study, retreats, and community life

Dzongpa Europa organizes a regular program of activities designed to bring the teachings of the Dharma and Tibetan culture closer to people from all walks of life. We combine open practices for those approaching for the first time with deeper spaces for regular practitioners and members.

Types of activities

Guided meditation

Regular meditation gatherings aimed at cultivating calm abiding, mindfulness, compassion, and inner clarity. Open to people with or without previous experience.

Dharma study

Introductory and study sessions on Tibetan Buddhism, traditional texts, the teachings of lineage masters, and the fundamental principles of the Buddhist path.

Cultural talks

Open activities on Tibetan culture, the history of Buddhism, sacred art, iconography, rituals, language, and the spiritual traditions of the Himalayas.

Retreats and teachings

Organization of retreats, courses, and teachings with qualified masters, both in Valencia and in other places in Europe, including the possibility of visits by Rinpoche.

Community and volunteering

Spaces for encounter among members, practitioners, and supporters, as well as opportunities to collaborate in organization, communication, translation, logistics, and support for activities.
