Purpose of the website

This website aims to present the activities of the Dzongpa Cultural Association and the Dzongpa Europa project, as well as to provide information about the Sakya Dzongpa lineage, the teachings of H.E. the VI Gongkar Dorje Dhenpa Rinpoche, and the ways to collaborate with the association.
