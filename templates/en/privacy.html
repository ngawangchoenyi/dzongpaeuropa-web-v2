Privacy policy

Data controller

Dzongpa Cultural Association, registered in the Register of Associations of the Valencian Community under number CV-01-062727-V, First Section. Tax ID G67651869, with registered office at C/ Pintor Navarro Llorens, 3-4, 46018 Valencia, and contact email secretaria@dzongpaeuropa.org.

Data we collect

Through the contact form, membership form, newsletter subscription, and donation processes, we may collect: name, surname, email address, telephone number, postal address, tax identification number (in the case of members or donors requesting a tax certificate), and bank or payment details.

Purpose of processing

To manage your request for information, membership, donation, or newsletter subscription; to send communications about the association's activities when you have given your consent; and to comply with applicable accounting, tax, and legal obligations.

Legal basis

Consent of the data subject, performance of the relationship with the member or donor, and compliance with legal obligations.

Retention

We will retain your data for the duration of the relationship and subsequently for the legally required periods (generally, up to six years for accounting and tax purposes).

Recipients

We will not transfer your data to third parties unless this is necessary for the provision of the service (banking entity, payment platform, newsletter mailing platform) or required by law.

Rights

You may exercise your rights of access, rectification, erasure, objection, restriction of processing, and portability at any time by writing to secretaria@dzongpaeuropa.org. You may also lodge a complaint with the Spanish Data Protection Agency (www.aepd.es).
