Weekly Pujas with Rinpoche from Taiwan

Every Saturday, H.E. Gongkar Dorje Dhenpa Rinpoche leads different pujas and lineage practices from the Dzongpa centre in Taiwan.

These activities allow the community to regularly connect with the blessings of the lineage and support Rinpoche's activities. The specific practice is usually announced 3 to 5 days in advance and prior registration is required.

About this practice

Dzongpa White Ganapati

Riqueza y prosperidad

Dzongpa White Ganapati is presented in the tradition as an uncommon practice associated with the increase of merit, prosperity and favourable conditions for life and Dharma practice.

Traditional benefits

  • Support the accumulation of merit and wisdom.
  • Help gather favourable conditions for practice and Dharma activities.
  • Dispel obstacles related to scarcity, lack of resources or instability.

Vajradaka Fire Puja

Puja de fuego

The Vajradaka Fire Puja is presented as a practice of deep purification, associated with the cleansing of negative karma and the elimination of inner and outer obstacles.

Traditional benefits

  • Purify negative karma and tendencies that disturb the mind.
  • Eliminate inner and outer obstacles.
  • Favour mental peace, tranquillity and continuity of practice.

How to participate

  1. The specific puja is announced a few days in advance.
  2. Dzongpa Europa updates the calendar and sends the corresponding notice.
  3. Interested participants register in advance.
  4. Registered participants receive the Zoom access information.
  5. Those who wish to may make the suggested donation for Rinpoche.

Regular practices

The pujas may include, among others, the following practices of the Dzongpa lineage:

☸

Green Tara

Practice of Mother Tara, source of protection and liberation from obstacles.

☸

Brahmanrupa Mahakala

Protector of the Dzongpa lineage for over 600 years, with a special bond with Rinpoche.

☸

Dzambala

Deity of abundance and spiritual and material wealth.

☸

White Ganapati Dzongpa

An uncommon practice of the Dzongpa lineage that increases wealth and perfects merit.

☸

Four-Armed Avalokiteshvara

The Great Compassionate One, a central practice for the benefit of all beings.

☸

Vajradaka Fire Puja

Fire puja to purify negative karma and remove inner and outer obstacles.

☸

Tsog Offering

Accumulation of merit and wisdom, restoration of commitments and vows.

Suggested donation for Rinpoche
