Become a member

Together, support the preservation of the lineage

Becoming a member of the Dzongpa Cultural Association is a direct way to support the growth of Dzongpa Europa and to help make the teachings of the Dharma available to more people.

Members' contributions make it possible to cover basic expenses, organize activities, invite masters, rent spaces, translate materials, maintain the website, and create a stable fund for future retreats and teachings.

Every contribution, large or small, helps create the conditions for Tibetan culture and the Buddhism of the Dzongpa lineage to flourish in our community.

Benefits for members

All members receive:

  • Access to the association's monthly newsletter.
  • Priority notices of activities, retreats, and visits by masters.
  • Access to the internal community channel for members.
  • Reduced rates for retreats and events with invited masters.
  • Annual certificate of contributions for income tax purposes.

Supporters, Protectors, and Benefactors also receive:

  • Reserved places at special events with limited capacity.
  • Optional recognition as supporters of the project.
  • Invitation to the annual meeting with the association's leadership.

Contributions may be modified or cancelled at any time by writing to secretaria@dzongpaeuropa.org. Each member may also make additional one-off contributions at any time.
