Donations

A contribution so that the teachings may flourish

Donations help sustain the activities of Dzongpa Europa and create the necessary conditions to preserve and share the teachings of the Dzongpa lineage.

Contributions may be used for the organization of teachings and retreats, visits by masters, translation of texts, rental of spaces, website maintenance, communication, practice materials, and the creation of a stable fund for the association.

Purpose of donations

  • Inviting lineage masters and organizing retreats.
  • In-person and online teachings.
  • Translation of Dharma texts into Spanish, English, and Chinese.
  • Rental of spaces for retreats and teachings.
  • Practice materials and library.
  • Website, communication, and newsletter.
  • Stable fund for the association.
  • Support for other cultural and spiritual projects of the lineage.

Non-monetary and asset donations

The association may also receive non-monetary or asset donations — movable goods, real estate, libraries, practice objects — provided that they are aligned with its cultural and spiritual aims and are accepted in accordance with the applicable regulations and the entity's internal agreements. If you wish to make a contribution of this kind, write to us at secretaria@dzongpaeuropa.org and we will guide you through the process.

Tax aspects

The Dzongpa Cultural Association is registered in the Register of Associations of the Valencian Community under number CV-01-062727-V (First Section). As a non-profit organization, we issue an annual certificate of contributions which may, where applicable, allow you to benefit from the tax deductions available for donations to non-profit entities. For this purpose, we need your full name, tax identification number, and postal address.
